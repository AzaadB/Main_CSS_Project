Favourite Series

Cobra Kai

It was created by Josh Heald and Jon Hurwitz.

It's distributed by Sony Pictures Television and is a sequel to The Karate Kid films by Robert Mark Kamen.

William Zabka and Ralph Macchio reprise their roles as Daniel LaRusso and Johnny Lawrence from The Karate Kid (1984) and its sequels (1986) and (1989).

In Cobra Kai, the original Karate Kid saga continues 34 years after The Next Karate Kid (1994).

Hawkeye

After Avengers: Endgame (2019), Clint Barton (Jeremy Renner) must partner with Kate Bishop (Hailee Steinfeld), to face enemies from his past as the Ronin in order to return home in time for the holidays.

Barton trains a 22-year-old Hawkeye fan to become his protégé, because Bishop dreams of becoming like him one day. In disguise as Ronin, she attracts Barton's attention.

Mr. IGLESIAS

Currently streaming on Netflix,

Mr. Iglesias is an American sitcom starring Gabriel Iglesias and executive produced by Kevin Hench, Joe Meloche, and Ron DeBlasio.

A second season of the show was announced in August 2019. It premiered on June 17, 2020. Due to health concerns, the series was canceled after two seasons in July 2021.
